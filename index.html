Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how I work, deliver projects, and ensure quality.

What’s your preferred iOS architecture?

I use MVVM or VIPER depending on the project’s complexity. For scalability and testability, I structure code with clean separation of concerns and reusable modules.

Can you integrate third-party APIs or SDKs?

Yes, I’ve worked with REST/GraphQL APIs, Firebase, Agora, RevenueCat, and Apple frameworks like MapKit, HealthKit, and CoreLocation.

Do you write unit or UI tests?

Absolutely. I use XCTest and XCUITest for automated testing. I follow TDD where needed, and cover business logic, networking, and UI flows.

How do you ensure app performance?

I profile apps using Xcode Instruments (Time Profiler, Allocations, Leaks), minimize memory usage, and follow best practices for smooth scrolling, async rendering, and efficient networking.

Are you comfortable with CI/CD pipelines?

Yes, I’ve used Jenkins, GitHub Actions, and fastlane to automate builds, testing, code signing, and TestFlight/App Store deployments.

Can you work with legacy code or Objective-C?

Yes. I’ve refactored and modernized legacy Objective-C apps by gradually migrating modules to Swift while maintaining app stability.
